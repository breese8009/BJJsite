Welcome to Big Break Jiu-jitsu.
We are a local family operated martial arts school that offers classes in Brazilian Jiu-jitsu and conditioning. We cater to all ages and currently offer classes for children between the ages of 4-6 and 7-12, teenagers and adults. Our classes range from beginner to advanced skill levels. Our goal is to teach practical and realistic self-defense as well as prepare for competition.
